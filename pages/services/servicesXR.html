Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between XR, AR, VR, and MR?

+
-

XR is the umbrella term. AR adds digital objects to the real world. VR is fully virtual. MR blends both—XR covers them all.

What devices can run XR experiences?

+
-

We develop for Oculus, HoloLens, Android/iOS phones, and web-based XR platforms.

Can XR be used for team collaboration?

+
-

Yes, XR enables virtual meetings, product reviews, and remote training with real-time interaction.

Is XR expensive?

+
-

We offer flexible pricing—from lightweight experiences to full-scale enterprise solutions—based on your goals and budget.

How long does it take to build an XR solution?

+
-

On average, it takes 3 to 8 weeks. It depends on how complex and interactive the experience is.
