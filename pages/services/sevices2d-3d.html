Our Animation Process

We follow a simple, collaborative process to bring your animation to life—step by step.

01. Brief & Concept
We understand your goals, audience, and style preferences.
  • Clarify project goals
  • Identify target audience
  • Discuss style and tone
  • Write a clear script
  • Create a visual storyboard
02. Script & Storyboard
We develop a clear script and create a visual storyboard.
03. Design & Animation
Our team designs characters, environments, and brings everything to life with smooth animations.
  • Design characters and backgrounds
  • Animate scenes and transitions
  • Add voiceover
  • Background music and sound effects
04. Voiceover & Sound
We add voiceovers, background music, and sound effects for an immersive experience.
05. Review & Delivery
You review the animation, suggest changes, and we deliver the final video in your desired format.
  • Client review and feedback
  • Final delivery in MP4/MOV or your chosen format

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between 2D and 3D animation?

+
-

2D animation is flat and best for explainer videos, while 3D animation gives depth and is ideal for realistic product or architectural visuals.

How long does it take to complete an animation project?

+
-

It depends on the complexity. A simple 2D explainer can take 1-2 weeks, while a detailed 3D animation may take 3-4 weeks.

Can I provide my own script or voiceover?

+
-

Yes, we can work with your script or voiceover, or help you create both.

Will I be able to suggest changes?

+
-

Of course! We offer revision rounds so you're happy with the final output.

What formats will I receive the final animation in?

+
-

You'll get high-quality MP4 or MOV files ready to use on websites, social media, or presentations.
